Fulfillment Integration’s

The entire process of fulfillment from receiving a customer’s order and sending it to a customer’s doorstep is a complex and lengthy process. A typical fulfillment process is inclusive of steps of:-

  • Receiving inventory shipments
  • Inventory storage
  • Order processing
  • Shipping
  • Returns processing

Order fulfillment can be an expensive and complex process and it has its set of challenges and complexities that every business face, while, delivering its goods to a customer’s doorstep.

Fulfillment Impact on Consumer Behavior and Business Returns

Inconsistent fulfillment and mistimed deliveries cause following changes into a buying pattern of a customer:-

  • Irregular fulfillment process further delays shipments, eventually, impacts business’s bottom line
  • Customers are reluctant to buy from a retailer having a negative delivery experience.
  • Customers prefer fast and moderate shipping options based on their budget.
  • Retailers not offering desirable options, risk losing business due unwillingness of customers.

However, a retailer cannot achieve optimum fulfillment, unless, he:-

  • Lower costs for shipping

  • Reduce operational costs

  • Expand its customer base, globally

  • Improve customer service

  • and Focus on selling

Challenges of Order fulfillment

To achieve the goals of maximum fulfillment efficiency by reducing operational and shipping costs, a retailer is required to carry out following steps:-

Inventory

Inventory management to avoid running out of stock situation to keep customers intact with a retailer.

planning

Demand planning to assess and maintain the levels of stocks in inventory to meet any sudden rise in demand.

Logistics

Logistics planning for effective management of shipping methods and speed to deliver package in time.

Supply chain

Supply chain execution to get depleted inventories refilled during unexpected rise in demand.

Fulfillment Integration to Improve Delivery Without Losing Time

Fulfillment integration is a method to bridge all the gaps existing into fulfillment process of a customer order. An integrated fulfillment across warehouses, storefronts and shipping sync order and inventory data uniformly for seamless packing, labeling and delivery of a customer order.

Through the medium of integrated fulfillment order delivery process become automated across different systems and processes involved in fulfillment. The automated fulfillment completely eliminates manual keying of customer and order into system and prevent data related error. Accuracy of data causes entire fulfillment process to be efficient and faster. In addition to efficiency, other benefits offered by fulfillment integration are:-

  • It helps to sync orders from multiple channels automatically based upon shipping dates and locations.
  • Process efficiency by saving time is the most important automation feature of an integrated fulfillment.
  • Being entire process automated and data accurate the shipping and delivery of orders become more timely and accurate.
  • Offers real time data visibility across customer, order and inventory data to make informed and intelligent business decisions.
  • Above all it offers a business scalability to handle as many as number of orders.
