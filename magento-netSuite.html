Why you should integrate your Magento Store with an NetSuite ERP solution?

An open source eCommerce store with robust data handling capabilities Magento brings an exceptional user experience and transaction behavior while selling a product from a Storefront. A flexible shopping cart, Magento offers a merchant complete control over the look, content, catalog and marketing-sales functionalities. Simple to install and provided with more than 50 payment gateways when integrated with NetSuite ERP, the efficiency of a Magento store increases several times, whether, it be fulfillment, order management or payment gateway.
