EDI Business Solutions to Meet a Specific Client Requirement

Though EDI is an exquisite way to bring data efficiency within an organization, but it is not a complete solution to each and every organization’s data management related query. Each individual business could have its own set of data exchange related issues that it resolves in its own way and method. WebBee offers the innovative EDI solution that matches to a specific need of an organization. The different types of EDI solutions offered by WebBee according to specific business requirements are:

Direct EDI

Also known to be as point-to-point EDI, it creates a single and straight connection between two business partners connected on internet through a specific protocol or set of protocol. Here the connection is established between each and every business partners, individually. Direct EDI is formed between large business houses to maintain connection with their suppliers.

EDI Network Services Provider

A better option to direct EDI model, an EDI network service provider also know to be as Value –Added Network (VAN) simplify data related complexities. VAN support the different and varying communication protocol used by different business partners and adequately supports the varying communication protocols.

EDI through AS2

Another most commonly known EDI communication model is through AS2. Under AS2 the data is transmitted over secure internet communication protocol. AS2 is based upon a client-server methodology while creating an envelope for EDI data that is communicated securely using digital certificates and encryption.

EDI through FTP/VPN, SFTP, FTPS

FTP over VPN, SFTP and FTPS are the most commonly used communication protocols for exchanging information through EDI documents through EDI on the Internet. These protocols can be used for connecting to a business partner directly or through EDI Network Services Provider.

Web EDI

Web based EDI range from non-integrated solutions for sending and receiving messages from web-accessible screens to models that automate basic integration processes for a limited number of messages. Different to EDI through AS2, Web EDI is being carried even through a standard internet browser.

EDI Outsourcing

Outsourced EDI help you to manage multiple tasks as well as B2B eCommerce solutions. Our management software has eased the process of error handling, control of automatic processes and control of communications. As an EDI outsourcing company, we, offer a great number of EDI resource for a company ongoing basis. Through outsourcing EDI, a business further can integrate Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP).

EDI Software

Only for internal usage EDI software remains behind the company firewall is preferred option, when company has internal resources to be implement and carry out EDI on the ongoing basis.
